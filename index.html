2025 — Built TaxMate

Every tax season felt like a scam — international students like us were paying filing companies half of what we’d get back. So we built our own. It was about community, trust, and self-reliance. I’m still proud of it.

SwiftUI + REST: Web app and iOS app for international students to upload W2s, extract data via OCR, and auto-generate Forms 8843/1040NR. Secure doc handling, multi-step user flow, free to use.

2025 — Built Jeevit

Inspired by MyFitnessPal and my own wellness journey, Jeevit wasn’t just a fitness tracker — it was a reflection of my need for balance, discipline, and mental clarity. I didn’t just build it. I needed it.

Built in SwiftUI with HealthKit + CloudKit. Features: manual and synced logging for water, food, weight, steps, and exercise. Local + iCloud persistence. Apple Sign-in. Full native graph rendering and custom dashboards.
